Who we are

A team of dedicated,
innovative and optimistic
people, working to
democratize access to
content.

People deserve a digital ecosystem that works for them, and with them.

Founded by Jim McKelvey, Co-founder of Block (formerly known as Square), and backed by Peter Thiel’s Founders Fund, Invisibly is building a future where people earn from their data and access content they love with no paywalls, no ads and no subscriptions.

While premium content traditionally can only be accessed through paid subscriptions, Invisibly is creating a marketplace to open up a new way to access content from top publishers and trusted journalism sources. People win by being able to access content on their terms, and publishers win by having an ethical way to collect reader data and generate new revenue streams from audiences who never seem to subscribe.

Our Values

We follow data to truth.

We’re not blue-sky thinkers, here only to talk about what’s possible. We’re here to make it happen. We have a data-centric culture where decision making is rooted in informed, pressure-tested and defendable results – allowing us to execute solutions we’re sure about.

We only answer to the people.

We’re bold, and we’re not sorry.

We’re relentlessly dedicated to
extraordinary talent.
